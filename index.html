    Game Rules

    LottoMax

    LottoMax is Canada’s exciting lottery game with jackpots up to $70 million! Here’s how it works:

    • Choose 7 numbers from 1 to 49 or use our generator.
    • Optionally include a bonus number for secondary prizes (e.g., match 6 + bonus).
    • Draws happen every Tuesday and Friday.
    • Match all 7 numbers to win the jackpot!
    • Smaller prizes are available for matching 3 or more numbers.

    Lotto 6/49

    Lotto 6/49 offers big jackpots and is one of Canada’s favorite lotteries! Here’s how it works:

    • Choose 6 numbers from 1 to 49 or use our generator.
    • Optionally include a bonus number for secondary prizes (e.g., match 5 + bonus).
    • Draws happen every Wednesday and Saturday.
    • Match all 6 numbers to win the jackpot!
    • Smaller prizes are available for matching 2 or more numbers.
